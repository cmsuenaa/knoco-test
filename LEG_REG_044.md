# Global Ingredient & Vegan Certification Registry
**Document Ref:** AERA-LEG-REG-044

**Department:** Legal and Compliance

**Status:** Corporate Compliance Record

## 1. Regulatory Purpose and Legal Mandate
This document serves as the central corporate authority verifying that 100% of the raw chemical compounds and biological botanical concentrates utilized across our production networks match our corporate charter requirements. It provides the strict evidentiary trail required to maintain international vegan, cruelty-free, and organic eco-label designations across all active jurisdictions.

## 2. Mandatory Verification and Traceability Standards
Chain of Custody Documentation: Every incoming batch of raw ingredients must be accompanied by an unbroken Chain of Custody (CoC) certificate identifying the exact farm or upcycled agricultural site of origin in Greece or other approved regional sources.

Molecular Origin Analysis: Analytical laboratory testing sheets must accompany every batch to legally prove the absence of any animal-derived proteins, secondary animal processing side-products, or cross-contamination from non-vegan processing facilities.

Component-Level Screening: Certification protocols require chemical transparency down to 0.001% of total compound weight, including processing aids, catalysts, and trace structural preservatives.

## 3. Registry Maintenance and Audit Cycles
Supplier Affidavits: Suppliers must sign legally binding annual affidavits confirming zero animal-derived components and zero structural changes to their production lines.

Re-Certification Protocols: The registry undergoes a full automated review every Q2. Any supplier failing to submit documentation within 15 business days of the deadline will face an immediate suspension of their commercial delivery clearance.
