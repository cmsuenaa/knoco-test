# Stability and Compatibility Report
**Document Ref:** AERA-RD-STAB-022

**Department:** Research & Development

**Status:** Technical Compliance Report

## 1. Scope of Testing
*This report evaluates the long-term chemical stability of AERA’s raw botanical compounds when housed inside minimalist, sustainable consumer-facing vessels.*

Testing focused on verifying product shelf-life without utilizing synthetic chemical stabilizers.

## 2. Vessel Material Specification
Tested Container Type: 100% Post-Consumer Recycled (PCR) high-purity aluminum tubes and medical-grade amber glass vials.

Internal Coating Specification: Food-safe, non-reactive interior lining inside aluminum structures to eliminate direct metal-to-emulsion contact.

## 3. Stress Test Parameters & Accelerated Aging Findings
Thermal Stress Inversions: Formulations were exposed to cycling temperatures ranging from -10°C to +50°C over a 90-day period.

UV Degradation Analysis: Amber glass and aluminum walls successfully prevented UV light penetration, ensuring zero degradation of the highly sensitive Bakuchiol complex.

Shelf-Life Estimation: Accelerated data models conclude that the formulation maintains 98.7% active molecular integrity over a 24-month period under standard storage settings, proving that elaborate secondary cardboard packaging is completely unnecessary.
