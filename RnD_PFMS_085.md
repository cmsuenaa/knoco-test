## 1. Document Purpose & Operational Guidelines
*This sheet serves as the primary scientific authority for the compound blending of AERA’s foundational topical solutions.*
In alignment with our structural formulation philosophy, all components are selected purely for their skin-barrier performance, excluding any artificial colors, synthetic fragrances, or ornamental texture-enhancers.

## 2. Core Liquid Concentrate Matrix (Batch: Ultra-Hydrate / Repair 2025)
Base Vehicle: 64.8% Pure Demineralized Aqua & Organic Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice.
Primary Active Phase A: 12.5% Bio-Identical Upcycled Vegan Peptides (Derived from Greek olive and grape agricultural byproducts).
Primary Active Phase B: 3.0% Stabilized Botanical Bakuchiol Complex (100% plant-derived Retinol substitute).
Secondary Skin Identical Lipids: 5.5% Vegan Ceramide NP, Cholesterol, and Squalane.
Humectant Complex: 10.0% Multi-weight Hyaluronic Acid & Vegetable Glycerin.
Natural Preservative System: 4.2% Plant-derived Glyceryl Caprylate and Pentylene Glycol.
Total Concentration: 100% Potency-Verified Functional Blend.

## 3. Compounding Specifications
Thermal Window: Blending must occur under strict cold-process parameters below 25°C to protect the heat-sensitive botanical peptide chains from structural breakdown.
pH Verification Standard: The final formulation batch must be stabilized to a skin-compatible pH level of $5.5 \pm 0.2$ prior to transfer into shipping containers.
