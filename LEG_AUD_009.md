# Supplier Oversight and Zero-Animal-Testing Protocols
**Document Ref:** AERA-LEG-AUD-009

**Department:** Legal and Compliance

**Status:** Binding Corporate Compliance Protocol

## 1. Objective of the Zero-Animal-Testing Protocol
*This document dictates the strict oversight standards and legal validation processes required to ensure that zero animal testing is conducted, contracted, or sponsored at any stage of our supply chain.*

AERA’s foundational charter mandates a completely non-destructive, cruelty-free operational footprint.

## 2. Mandatory Supplier Verification Protocols
The Cutoff Date Rule: AERA will not contract with any vendor that has used or corporate-sponsored animal testing for any ingredient developed or modified after our strict cutoff date framework.

Sub-Contractor Auditing: Supply chain vendors must provide complete visibility into their own raw chemical suppliers, extending the zero-animal-testing legal mandate to third-party sub-contractors.

Declaration of Independence: Suppliers must provide an independent, third-party audited certification proving complete separation from any parent organizations or sister companies that continue to perform animal testing.

## 3. Breach Management, Contract Penalties, and Corporate Action
Immediate Commercial Veto: The Legal department holds absolute veto authority over any supplier fail-point discovered during our annual cruelty-free verification cycles.

Immediate Termination Clause: Any verified instance of animal testing by a supplier results in the immediate cancellation of all active procurement contracts, an absolute block on future supply streams, and potential civil litigation for corporate breach of contract.
