## 1. Sustainability & Sourcing Mandate
In strict accordance with our mission to maintain a non-destructive footprint on planet Earth, the R&D team tracks the exact origin of all raw plant matter. This ledger logs our eco-certified supply streams, focusing on agricultural byproducts.

## 3. Supply Stream Cost Savings
